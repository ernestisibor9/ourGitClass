Hala Madrid!

Madrid is the capital and most populous city of Spain, with a population of over 3.3 million people. It is the country's most populous urban area, with more than 2.1 million inhabitants in the city proper and 1.3 million in the metropolitan area. Madrid is the second most populous city in Europe after Barcelona. The city is known for its stunning architecture, including the Sagrada Familia Cathedral, the Palacio Real, and the España Española Museum. Madrid has been a major center for art, fashion, and culture since the 19th century.
